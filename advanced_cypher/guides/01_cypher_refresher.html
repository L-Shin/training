Task: Complex Graph Query


Find all Actors and Movies they acted in Whose name starts with "T" Aggregate their activity and movie titles Filter by who acted in more than 5 movies Return their name, birth-year and movie-titles Ordered by number of movies Limited to top 10

In SQL


SELECT a.name, a.born,
       group_concat(m.title) as movies,
       count(*) as cnt
FROM   actors as a JOIN actor_movie ON  (a.id = actor_movie.actor_id)
JOIN   movies as m
  ON  (actor_movie.movie_id = m.id)
WHERE  a.name LIKE "T%"
GROUP BY a.name, a.born
HAVING cnt > 5 ORDER BY cnt DESC

Cypher Query Structure


MATCH pattern
WHERE predicate
RETURN/WITH expression AS alias ...
ORDER BY expression
SKIP ... LIMIT ...
